Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

🔹 How do I change my profile picture? ➕

Go to the Settings Page, upload a new picture, and click "Save".

🔹 How do I enable Dark Mode? ➕

Visit the Settings Page, and toggle the "Dark Mode" switch.

🔹 How do I contact support? ➕

You can email us at support@example.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567.

🔹 How do I reset my password? ➕

Click "Forgot Password" on the Login Page, and follow the instructions.

🔹 How do I add new team members? ➕

Navigate to the Team Page, click "Add Member," enter their details, and assign their roles.

🔹 How do I upload and manage files? ➕

Go to the Dashboard, select the "File Management" section, and upload files by dragging them into the designated area.

🔹 How can I track project progress? ➕

Check the Dashboard for an overview of ongoing tasks, project milestones, and real-time status updates.

🔹 How do I manage tasks efficiently? ➕

Use the Task Management section to create tasks, assign them to team members, set deadlines, and track progress.

🔹 How do I recover deleted files? ➕

Deleted files are temporarily stored in the "Trash" folder for 30 days. You can restore them from the File Management section.

🔹 How do I enable email notifications for updates? ➕

Go to Settings, navigate to "Notifications," and enable email alerts for task assignments, file uploads, and system updates.

🔹 How does version control work in file management? ➕

The system automatically saves different versions of files. You can access previous versions in the File Management section under "Version Control."

🔹 Why is my dashboard loading slowly? ➕

Check your internet connection, clear your browser cache, and ensure your browser is updated. If issues persist, contact support.
